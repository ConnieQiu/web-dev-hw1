Taylor Swift through the Eras

Taylor Swift is a record breaking pop artist, releasing 10 albums over her 16 year career so far. She started as a country music artist and is now one of the most well known pop artists in the world. She prides herself in writing most of her own songs and lyrics with fan favorites such as "All Too Well (10 Minute Version)", Blank Space, and her newest hit Anti-Hero.

Biography:

Taylor Swift was born on December 13, 1989 in West Reading, Pennsylvania. Taylor was interested in music from a young age, performing at coffee shops and going to New York City for singing lessons. She eventually got interested in country music and moved to Nashville with her family to pursue her career. She started her country music career by releasing her first album named after herself "Taylor Swift". Taylor would continue to pursue country music until her album "Red" where she started incorporating more pop sounding sounds in her albums. She finally decided to be a pop artist with the release of her album "1989". Taylor has also experimented with other genres such as indie pop with her two albums "Folklore" and "Evermore". Throughout her career she has broken many records with her music and is currently on a completely sold out tour, "The Eras Tour".
